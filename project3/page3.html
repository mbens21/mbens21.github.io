Athletics Marketing

Social Media Design
As a student graphic designer working for the Athletics Marketing Department, I use Adobe Programs to produce graphics that promote varsity athletics on social media. I primarily focus on men’s hockey and men’s lacrosse, while occasionally helping out with football.
The first graphic was displayed on a video board as fans took pictures in front of it. The next two "4 Tickets for 40" graphics promote ticket plans, one on Twitter and one as an advertisement in the South Bend Tribune. The fourth image was posted on Twitter for the game against Michigan in Notre Dame stadium. The last graphic was posted on Facebook to promote "flex plans" available for hockey fans.
